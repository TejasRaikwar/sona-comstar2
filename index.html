Recent/Important Notices

  • Sona Comstar entered into an agreement to acquire Escorts Kubota's Railway Equipment Division for ₹1,600 crore. This strategic move is part of the company's efforts to diversify its mobility offerings beyond the automotive sector

  • : As of December 2024, Sona Comstar is actively engaging with automakers in China, Japan, and South Korea to supply components for electric, hybrid, and internal combustion engine vehicles.

  • The company aims for these markets to contribute over half of its revenues within the next five years, shifting focus from its traditional markets in North America and Europe.

  • In the first half of the fiscal year 2025, Sona Comstar added two new EV programs and onboarded two new customers, bringing its total awarded programs to 36.
